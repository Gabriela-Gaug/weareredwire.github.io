Hello!

We are Redwire. Our mission is to grow together as a team by actively investing in people and to ultimately take on any challenge and bring to life projects we truly believe in and are passionate about.

We do code

  • Software Development

    Our vision of the current Software-as-a-Service trend is different from others. We believe that, wherever possible, human intervention should be removed, such as in overseeing the development process. The act of management should be emergent from the process itself, as is maintaining a clear and permanent channel of communication to our client.

  • Cloud Solutions

    We have an array of experienced developers that are familiar with cloud infrastructures and distributed systems. We can add value to your existing project by porting it to the cloud and improve its performance and availability through various techniques that will help you take your application to the next level of integration and excellent interoperability.

  • Web Design

    We provide solutions that will establish a visual identity for your software, based on your requirements and our expertise in defining visual concepts and graphical user interfaces and experience, usable in both mobile and desktop environments. All of our designs are developed through research and discovery of competitors and their practical implementations, to make your project stand out from all the others on the web.

What's in the toolbox

  • C#

    World-class language designed from the bottomup to be compliant with pretty much any complex business scenario that it might be required to fulfil.

  • PHP

    The world’s rst approach for building websites. It is fast, runs on pretty much anything, and in the hands of a talented codesmith, can work wonders for your business.

  • C++

    C++ is a very powerful language. C++ allows you to have a lot of control as to how you use computer resources, so in the right hands its speed and ability to cheaply use resources should be able to surpass other languages. Thanks to it’s performance, we can use it to develop real-time systems on multiple platforms.

  • Data Storage

    We specialize in various types of data storage, ranging from traditional SQL-oriented tech to more modern document or graph-oriented structures, whilst adding to them our experience with Big Data.

  • NodeJS

    Node.js is a platform built on Chrome’s JavaScript runtime for easily building fast and scalable network applications. Node.js uses an eventdriven, non-blocking I/O model that makes it lightweight and e icient, perfect for data-intensive real-time applications that run across distributed devices.

  • JavaScript

    From complex user interfaces and interactions to asynchronous access, javascript is the language that brings life to your browser and is now a widely used tool for single page applications and realtime systems.

  • Java

    Java was once the undisputed king of the business application software languages. It includes all the major bells and whistles, ranging from crossplatform to a multi- paradigm mentality.

  • IoT

    The Internet of Things is about the seamless interaction between anything from machine to machine to machine to humans, all revolving around 3 base principles: sensing, controlling, and interacting.

  • Mobile Development

    We are prepared with a full arsenal of knowledge for the development of apps for your Android/IoS/ Windows device using the best tools available on the market to create the best user experience you want to o er your users.
